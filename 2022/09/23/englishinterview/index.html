Introduce yourself？介绍下你自己？

  • 我叫董梦龙，现就读于安徽农业大学计算机科学与技术专业，我前三学年平均绩点3.68/5.0，专业排名第二，曾获得专业一等奖学金、“三好学生”称号等荣誉，并且以553和440的成绩通过全国大学生英语四六级考试，为我在硕士阶段的深造打下了坚实基础。

  • 本科期间，我在科研、竞赛、实践方面均有所涉猎。我主持和参与了四项大学生创新创业项目（其中有两项省级），并且申请了两项国家发明专利和一项软件著作，参与了一篇EI论文的撰写。我积极参与学科竞赛，在本科期间获得了五个国家级奖项和十一个省级奖项。

  • 我曾主持过一项校级暑期“三下乡”社会实践活动，并且担任学院学生党支部组织委员，尽具有较强的实践和工作能力。我的介绍到此结束，谢谢老师。

    • My name is Dong Menglong, and I am now majoring in Computer Science and Technology in Anhui Agricultural University. In the first three years, my GPA is 3.68/5.0, ranking the second in my major. I have won the first-class scholarship of my major and the title of “Merit Student”. Moreover, I passed the CET-4 and CET-6 with scores of 553 and 440, which laid a solid foundation for my further study in the master’s stage.

    • During my undergraduate study, I dabbled in scientific research, competition and practice. I have presided over and participated in four undergraduate innovation and entrepreneurship projects, applied for two national invention patents and a software work, and participated in the writing of an EI paper. I actively participated in discipline competitions and won five national awards and eleven provincial awards during my undergraduate study.

    • I once presided over a university-level summer “three rural” social practice activity, and served as the committee member of the college student Party branch, so I have strong practice and work ability. This is the end of my introduction. Thank you.

Introduce your major？介绍你的专业

  • 我大学学的专业是计算机科学与技术，我们主要学习了面向对象程序设计、数据结构与算法、操作系统、计算机网络等专业课程，通过以上课程，可以让我系统掌握计算机硬件、软件的基本理论与应用基本技能，我非常热爱我的专业。计算机技术在日常生活中应用广泛，所以我想利用这些技术造福社会。
  • My major in university is computer science and technology, we mainly study object-oriented programming, data structures and algorithms, operating systems, computer networks and other professional courses, through the above courses, I can systematically master the basic theory and application of computer hardware and software basic skills, I love my major very much. Computer technology is widely used in daily life, so I want to use these technologies to benefit society.

Introduce your college？介绍你的大学？

  • 我在安徽农业大学学习。尽管它。虽然不是很了解，但我仍然很欣赏它，因为它给了我一个发展自己能力的机会。学校位于安徽省合肥市，是安徽省人民政府共同建设的省级重点大学。
  • l studied in Anhui Agricultural University. Although it is not well know, I still appreciate it, because it offers me a chance to develop my abilities. The university is located in Hefei City, Anhui Province. It is a provincial key university jointly established by Anhui Provincial People’s Government.

Introduce your hometown？介绍你的家乡？

  • 我的家乡在亳州，这是华佗和曹操的故乡。亳州以古井贡酒和中草药而闻名。亳州市拥有中国最大的中药贸易中心，是一座有着深厚历史底蕴的城市。
  • My hometown is in Bozhou City, which is the hometown of Hua Tuo and Cao Cao. Bozhou City is famous for its 古井 wine and Chinese herbal medicine. Bozhou City has the largest Chinese medicine trade center in China, and it is a city with profound historical deposits.

Introduce your reason for graduate school？介绍你读研的原因？

  • 在过去的三年里，我学到了很多专业知识和实践技能，但渐渐地，我意识到这是不够的。在我看来，进一步的学习其实是我迫切需要认识到和。最终实现自我价值。生命是宝贵的，必须抓住任何自我发展的机会，尤其是在竞争激烈的现代社会。因此，我更愿意继续深造。
  • During the past three years, l have learned a lot of professional knowledge and practical skills,but gradually，I realize it is not enough. In my opinion，further study is actually urgent for me to realize and.finally achieve self-value. Life is precious,it is necessary to catch any opportunity for self-development，especially in the competitive modern society. Therefore,l prefer to go on for further education.

why you choose our university?为什么选择我们学校

  • 原因有很多。 首先，南京理工大学是双一流建设高校，它可以为学生提供丰富的学习资源。 此外，通过我在研究生阶段的学习，我发现做研究有很多有趣的事情可以做，做研究是一种乐趣。
  • There are many reasons. First of all, Nanjing University of Science and Technology is a double-first-class university, which can provide students with abundant learning resources. In addition, through my study in the postgraduate stage, I found that doing research has many interesting things to do, doing research is a kind of fun.

  • 我无法表达我的歉意，但我真的不知道，我得看看。
  • l can’t tell you how sorry l am, but l really don’t know, l will have to check.
